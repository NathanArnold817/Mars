Heading to the red planet

The New Frontier

Sixteen years ago after the fifth world war and disease and famine plaguing the earth organizations were put into place. Ones that will forever alter the course of mankind.

What To Expect
  • Temperatures ranging from 55-85 degrees
  • Artificial sunlight and atmosphere
  • Vegetation grown throughout the planet
  • Completely terraformed; with bodies of water so bring a suit
  • A free republic with no governement or leadership
